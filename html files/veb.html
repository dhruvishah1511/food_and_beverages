Veg Biryani Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, cauliflower), diced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Whole spices: 1 bay leaf, 2-3 cloves, 2-3 green cardamoms, 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fried onions for garnish (optional)
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add the whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add diced vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Now, add yogurt, mint leaves, coriander leaves, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Drain the soaked basmati rice and add it to the pot. Mix gently.
  8. Add 3 cups of water (adjust according to rice quality), cover the pot, and let it cook until the rice is almost done.
  9. If using saffron, sprinkle saffron-infused milk on top of the rice.
  10. Cover and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  11. Garnish with fried onions (optional) and serve hot with raita or salad.

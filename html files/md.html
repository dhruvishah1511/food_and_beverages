Methi Dal Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal), rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup fenugreek leaves (methi), washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook yellow lentils with turmeric powder, salt, and enough water until soft and mushy.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  6. Add chopped fenugreek leaves and cook until they wilt.
  7. Add red chili powder and mix well.
  8. Now, add the cooked lentils and adjust the consistency by adding water if necessary.
  9. Simmer for a few minutes until the flavors blend together.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with rice or roti.

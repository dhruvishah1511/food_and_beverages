Delicious Masala Bhindi Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 500g fresh okra (bhindi)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry the okra, then cut off the ends and slit them vertically without cutting through.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add slit okra to the pan and mix gently to coat the masala.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the okra is tender.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with roti or rice.

Indian-Style Vegetable Casserole Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, carrots, peas, cauliflower), diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes until they are partially cooked.
  8. Transfer the vegetable mixture to a casserole dish.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and chopped coriander leaves on top.
  10. Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Once done, remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
  12. Serve hot with rice or bread.

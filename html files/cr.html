Curd Rice Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice with water until soft and mushy. Let it cool slightly.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt and milk until smooth. Add this mixture to the cooked rice and mix well. The rice should be coated well with the yogurt mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add cumin seeds, chopped green chilies, grated coconut (if using), and curry leaves. Saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add this tempering to the rice mixture and mix well.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Curd rice is ready to be served. You can serve it warm or chilled.

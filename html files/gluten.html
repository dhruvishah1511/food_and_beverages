Chicken enchiladas
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup firm tofu, cubed
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add tofu and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and green onions. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds.
Loaded vegetable quesadillas
Ingredients:
  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook bacon until crispy. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain cooked pasta and immediately toss with bacon and garlic mixture.
  5. Quickly add egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly until creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve hot.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Recipes?

Health Benefits

Experience improved digestion and overall well-being with gluten-free recipes.

Weight Management

Achieve your weight goals with gluten-free recipes that are often lower in calories and carbs.

Energy Boost

Enjoy sustained energy levels throughout the day with gluten-free meals.

Gluten Sensitivity

Avoid discomfort and symptoms associated with gluten sensitivity or intolerance.

Celiac Disease Management

Manage celiac disease and maintain a gluten-free diet for better health outcomes.

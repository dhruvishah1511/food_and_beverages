Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 250g ground beef or pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
  • 400g canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or pork to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Add grated carrots and chopped celery to the skillet. Cook for a few minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in canned diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, red chili flakes (if using), and red wine (if using). Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  7. Serve spaghetti topped with Bolognese sauce. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

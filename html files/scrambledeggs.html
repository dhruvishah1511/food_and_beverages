Delicious Scrambled Eggs Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Chopped chives or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with milk, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted and hot.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them cook without stirring for a few seconds until they start to set on the bottom.
  4. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked eggs from the edges towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Continue cooking and gently stirring until the eggs are softly set and slightly runny in places.
  6. Remove from heat immediately when the eggs are still slightly underdone, as they will continue to cook from residual heat.
  7. Transfer the scrambled eggs to a plate immediately to prevent overcooking.
  8. Garnish with chopped chives or parsley if desired.
  9. Serve hot with toast or as desired.

Indian-Spiced Roasted Vegetables Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 large bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 small eggplant, diced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine diced potatoes, carrots, bell pepper, cauliflower florets, diced eggplant, sliced onion, and minced garlic.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper to the vegetables. Mix well until the vegetables are evenly coated with the spices.
  5. Spread the seasoned vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned, stirring halfway through the cooking time.
  7. Once roasted, remove from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  8. Serve hot as a side dish or with rice or bread.

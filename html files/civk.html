Creamy indian Vegetable Kurma Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes), diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut, grated
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1-inch ginger, chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, grind grated coconut, green chilies, ginger, and garlic to a smooth paste using little water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the coconut paste and mix everything together.
  8. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency of the kurma.
  9. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the vegetables are cooked through.
  10. Add garam masala and curry leaves. Mix well.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  12. South Indian Style Vegetable Kurma is ready to be served with rice, roti, or dosa.

Classic Caesar Salad Recipe

----Gallery----

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large salad bowl, add chopped romaine lettuce.
  2. Pour Caesar salad dressing over the lettuce.
  3. Add grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add croutons.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss everything together until the lettuce is evenly coated with the dressing.
  7. Serve immediately as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal.
